Our Mission

Protégé intents to reduce reliance on foreign vendors by developing software, and building platforms and ecosystems for government, corporations and institutions in South Africa; having these products and services build and supplied by South Africans, in South Africa.

Our Goal

Our goal is to build software solutions that integrate and merge into platforms, and platforms that overlap to form ecosystems, uniquely adapted for South African businesses and institutions in the motor, finance, logistics, mining, insurance and medical industries, amongst others.

We believe that all organizations have a degree of disconnect between their business processes and their IT systems

These disconnects introduce admin lags, a source of inefficiencies and delays. Do you experience issues when the admin is not up to date?

We believe that IT is there to serve an organization, not to be served

Do you feel that you are constrained by your IT systems? Or that IT dictates how your department should run?

Our value propositions

Identify the disconnect
